THE LOST GALLERY / generative poster design

Data driven poster generator about Art Theft, made with OPENRNDR and Density Design in Kotlin language. The paintings showned have been stolen and then lost for some time now. It’s possible to see only a small portion of the unrecovered paintings, that is inversely proportional to the time passed since their theft. 

Sources:
"List of stolen paintings" Wikipedia, "Cultural heritage crime/ Stolen Works of Art Database" Interpol , Artstor Library , Guide OpenRNDR .
