OUTFORIA / interaction design

Outforia is an App that suggest to the users activities based on his interest, actioniong when the user is crossing the line, using too much social networks. The mission is the re educations of teenagers to the use of smartphone and social media in order to increase the awarness of the user personal way to live the technology. Outforia evokes the good feeling of euforia that the user can feel when discovering new things, when he understand how to live in an authentic way the real world.

This number 0 is called Virtual Identity and navigate through the issues of new generations online.
