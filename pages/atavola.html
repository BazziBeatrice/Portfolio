BEATRICE BAZZAN INDEX CONTACT

A TAVOLA* / poster design

Poster project for an awareness campaign about ocean pollution caused by plastic waste. The concept presented here is based on the vision of a possible, and not so far, future in which the fish will be as synthetic as a piece of clothing, that it will need to be labeled, in order to report its contents back. The label is directly involved by the big star following the ironic slogan Buon Appetito, that means enjoy your meal.

year: 2018 / 2019

art direction: HELMUT LANGER

team: Beatrice Bazzan / Antontella Autuori / Elena Catani / Costanza Trinari
